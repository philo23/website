2Prowl

Quickly send a webpage to your iPhone, iPod or iPad for viewing, using Prowl (iTunes App Store) to push a notification to your iOS device. Swiftly move from your desktop or laptop to your mobile device without having to tap out those huge URLs on the touch screen keyboard ever again. Now with automatic redirects! (Prowl 1.2+ required)
